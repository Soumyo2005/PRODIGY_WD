Green Tea

Known for its high antioxidant content and metabolism-boosting properties. Our organic green tea is sourced from the finest tea gardens and gently processed to preserve its natural benefits.

  • Rich in EGCG antioxidants
  • May support weight management
  • Gentle caffeine content
  • Fresh, grassy flavor profile

Herbal Tea

Caffeine-free infusions made from herbs, flowers, and spices. Each blend is crafted for specific wellness benefits and delightful flavors.

  • Naturally caffeine-free
  • Wide variety of flavors
  • Targeted wellness benefits
  • Perfect any time of day

Black Tea

Fully oxidized leaves produce a robust flavor and higher caffeine content. Our black teas are rich and satisfying.

  • Bold, full-bodied flavor
  • Higher caffeine content
  • Rich in theaflavins
  • Great with milk or lemon

White Tea

The least processed tea with delicate flavors and subtle sweetness. Hand-picked young leaves and buds.

  • Delicate, subtle flavors
  • Highest antioxidant levels
  • Lowest caffeine content
  • Rare and precious

Perfect Brewing Guide

1

Quality Water

Start with fresh, filtered water for the best flavor

2

Proper Temperature

Different teas require specific water temperatures

3

Measure Carefully

Use about 1 teaspoon per 8oz cup

4

Steep Time

Follow recommended steeping times for each tea type

Ideal Brewing Temperatures

  • White Tea: 160-180°F (71-82°C)
  • Green Tea: 175-185°F (79-85°C)
  • Oolong Tea: 185-205°F (85-96°C)
  • Black Tea: 200-212°F (93-100°C)
  • Herbal Tea: 205-212°F (96-100°C)
